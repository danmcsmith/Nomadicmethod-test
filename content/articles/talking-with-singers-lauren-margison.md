Young Canadian soprano [Lauren Margison](https://twitter.com/LaurenMargison) has lived her life surrounded by opera and the people who make it. The daughter of renowned Canadian tenor [Richard Margison](http://www.richardmargison.com/)Â and of violist and stage director [Valerie Kuinka](http://www.margisonkuinka.com/#about), Lauren has an unique perspective on the operatic industry. She spoke with [Schmopera](http://www.schmopera.com) about the life she's seen, the big name she brings with her, and how she carves out her own identity as a singer.
